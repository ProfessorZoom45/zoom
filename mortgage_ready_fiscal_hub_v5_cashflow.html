How to Read the Weekly Table

What “leftover” means

Leftover is money after scheduled calendar items only. It does not automatically include food, gas, random spending, or emergencies. That is why the plan uses a $150 cushion.

What to do on yellow weeks

Do not panic. Yellow means the bills fit, but the margin is thin. Work overtime if possible, reduce eating out, delay optional spending, and avoid extra credit payments beyond the calendar amount.

What to do on red weeks

Red means the base $775 income does not cover planned scheduled items. You either need overtime/extra income, to move a non-critical payment, or temporarily lower optional savings/extra credit attack.
